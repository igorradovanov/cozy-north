Good typography shouldn't make you think. It should feel like settling into your favorite chair with a warm drink, where the words simply flow and your eyes never strain.

Body text is optimized at 15px with a 1.65 line-height for maximum comfort. The max-width of 68ch keeps lines from getting too long to track comfortably.
